Research Study

Spectral — Music & Mood

You'll listen to the same track in three different versions. After each, rate how it made you feel. Takes about 10 minutes.

🎧 Headphones recommended 🔇 Find a quiet place ⏱ ~10 minutes
Before You Begin

Welcome

This study investigates whether the frequency balance of a piece of music — how bright or dark it sounds — can shift how you feel emotionally, independent of the melody or rhythm.

What You'll Do

You'll listen to one song played in three different versions — Sad, Neutral, and Happy. Each version has the same melody and rhythm, but different EQ processing applied.

After each version you'll answer a few quick questions about how it made you feel. Your responses are anonymous.

ℹ Please use headphones if possible — the EQ differences are much more noticeable than on laptop speakers.
